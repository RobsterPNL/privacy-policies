Privacy Policy

Habit Tracker

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Overview

Habit Tracker ("the App") is a habit tracking application for Android. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your choices.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Habit definitions (name, type, schedule, reminder time)
  • Completion entries (date, value)
  • User progress (XP, level, streaks, achievements)
  • App settings (theme preference, language preference)

This data is never transmitted to any server. It stays on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

Data Collected by Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services provided by Google:

Firebase Analytics

We collect anonymous usage data to understand how the App is used and to improve it. This includes:

  • App opens and screen views
  • Feature usage events (e.g., habit completed, achievement unlocked, level reached)
  • Device type, operating system version, and language
  • Country/region (derived from IP address, which is not stored)

This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally. Analytics is enabled by default when you use the App. You can learn more at Firebase Privacy.

Firebase Crashlytics

We collect anonymous crash reports to identify and fix bugs. This includes:

  • Crash logs and stack traces
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App state at the time of the crash

This data is anonymous and is used solely to improve App stability. Crash reporting is enabled by default when you use the App. You can learn more at Firebase Privacy.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any personal information
  • We do not collect your location
  • We do not require an account or login
  • We do not share or sell any data to third parties

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal data from children under 13. The App does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
